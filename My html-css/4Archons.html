Zhongli Geo Archon

Zhongli is a 5-star Geo Archon who uses polearm weapon, best used in a support role for many different teams compositions due to his highly effective-shielding capabilities. He is also a consultant of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor.

Raiden Shogun Electro Archon

The Raiden Shogun is the Electro Archon and supreme ruler of the island nation of Inazuma, referred to as lightning itself. She is an Electro Polearm user who supports her allies with her consistent lightning attacks and elemental burst buffs to her allies.

Nahida Dendro Archon

Nahida is the current Dendro Archon, also known as Lesser-Lord Rusanali, and was introduced as a playable character in Genshin. She uses her signicture weapon the "A Thousand Floating Dreams."
